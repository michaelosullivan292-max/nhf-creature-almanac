Nocturnal Haven Forest Opposition Almanac

A guide for identifying, understanding, and surviving the opposition in Nocturnal Haven Forest.

The Sender Man

Class: Creatures

A mysterious mail carrier figure connected to unsettling letters, strange packages, and broken forest supply routes.

The Shovel

Class: Creatures

A fast forest stalker connected to isolated camps, missing gear, and sudden encounters in the dark woods.

The Skinwalker

Class: Creatures

A shapeshifting forest entity known for confusing patrols, copying forms, and creating uncertainty in the woods.

The Hidebehind

Class: Creatures

A tall forest lurker known for hiding behind trees and avoiding direct sight.

The Wendigo

Class: Creatures

A winter-associated forest entity tied to hunger, famine conditions, and old cursed territory.

The Montana Sleep Experiment Test Subjects

Class: Creatures

Experiment survivors altered by the black site event and often connected to missing supplies and sleepless patrol reports.

Mason Moor Reese

Class: Creatures

A masked lake guardian connected to Emerald Lake, campsite warnings, and strange nighttime sightings.

Miguel Marez

Class: Creatures

A masked mechanic figure tied to an old forest home and Halloween reports.

The Cursed Trees

Class: Creatures

Rare living trees that move, block routes, and make the forest itself feel unsafe.

VHS Express

Class: Creatures

A vanished rental store that appears after midnight with strange rules, old tapes, and an unsettling calm around it.

Loot Goblins

Class: Creatures

Small forest scavengers known for grabbing supplies and running off with sacks.

Survival Tips

Travel in groups, keep supplies organized, avoid unknown containers, respect marked areas, and pay attention to weather changes.
